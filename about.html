About

I’m Markus — a former ship captain turned surf coach and mountain wanderer. I guide calm, small-group days between the sea and the Arctic mountains.

The idea

Illusia Adventures is about moving slow enough to actually be there. We build confidence through clear steps, simple decisions, and days that feel safe.

Most days are quiet in the best way: a few people, a plan that fits the conditions, and space to breathe.

Safety, simply

Safety is a practice: conservative choices, calm communication, and adjusting the plan when needed. You’ll always know what we’re doing — and why.
